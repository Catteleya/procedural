How to - Breathe


Do you want to breathe? Well, Read these instructions to learn to breathe!

1)
First, Get comfortable. You can lie on your back in bed or on the floor with a pillow under your head and knees.

2)
Secondly, Breathe in through your nose. Let your lungs fill with air.

3)
Next, Breathe out through your nose.

4)
After that, Place one hand on your chest.

5)
As you breathe in, feel your chest and lungs rise.

6)
And then dont stop breathing!!

The one step version!
